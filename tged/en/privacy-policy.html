Privacy Policy

Detail Games is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and process your personal information when you use our services.

1. Purpose of Personal Information Collection and Use

Detail Games collects and uses personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service provision and operation
  • User identification and authentication
  • Customer support and inquiry response
  • Service improvement and new service development
  • Marketing and advertising

2. Types of Personal Information Collected

Required Information

  • Email address
  • Nickname or username
  • Password (stored encrypted)

Optional Information

  • Profile picture
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Other profile information

3. Methods of Personal Information Collection

  • Direct input during registration
  • Automatic collection during service use (cookies, logs, etc.)
  • Customer support inquiries

4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Detail Games will destroy personal information without delay after the purpose of collection and use has been achieved. However, if preservation is required by relevant laws, the information will be retained for the specified period.

5. Provision of Personal Information to Third Parties

Detail Games does not provide personal information to third parties in principle. However, exceptions may be made in the following cases:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law or when requested by investigative agencies in accordance with procedures and methods prescribed by law for investigative purposes

6. Personal Information Processing Outsourcing

Detail Games may outsource personal information processing to improve services. When outsourcing, we ensure that service providers comply with personal information protection requirements, maintain confidentiality of personal information, and prohibit provision to third parties through outsourcing contracts in accordance with relevant laws.

7. Security Measures for Personal Information

Detail Games takes the following measures to ensure the security of personal information:

  • Personal information encryption
  • Technical measures against hacking
  • Access restrictions to personal information
  • Storage and prevention of access log tampering
  • Regular backup of personal information

8. User Rights and Exercise Methods

Users may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request to view personal information processing status
  • Request correction or deletion in case of errors
  • Request suspension of processing

9. Personal Information Protection Officer

Personal Information Protection Officer: Detail Games Privacy Team

Contact: dev01@detailgames.ai

If you have any complaints or inquiries regarding personal information processing, please contact us at any time.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised in accordance with changes in relevant laws and guidelines or company policies. When revised, we will notify you through our website.

This Privacy Policy is effective from January 1, 2024.
