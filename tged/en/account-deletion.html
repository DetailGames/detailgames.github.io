Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your Detail Games account, please follow the instructions below.

⚠️ Important Notice

Account deletion is an irreversible action. When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Profile information and settings
  • Game progress and saved data
  • Purchase history and payment information
  • Friends list and social features data
  • All other personal data

Account Deletion Process

To delete your account, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Send Email

Send an account deletion request to the following email address:

Account Deletion Request Email Address:

dev01@detailgames.ai

Subject: [Account Deletion Request] Username or Email

Step 2: Email Content

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Subject: [Account Deletion Request] Username or Email
  • Body:
    • Email address of the account to be deleted
    • Username (nickname)
    • Reason for account deletion (optional)
    • Confirmation of consent for account deletion

Step 3: Processing Period

After receiving your account deletion request, it will be processed within the following period:

  • Account deletion processed within 3-5 business days
  • Confirmation notification sent via email upon completion
  • Separate inquiry possible for urgent cases

Pre-Deletion Checklist

Before deleting your account, please check the following:

  • Ensure important game data is backed up
  • Check for any ongoing payments or subscription services
  • Check for accounts linked to other services
  • Understand that recovery is not possible after account deletion

After Account Deletion

After your account is deleted:

  • You will not be able to log in to the service with that account
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted
  • You can create a new account with the same email
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered

Alternative: Account Deactivation

Instead of complete account deletion, you can temporarily deactivate your account:

  • When deactivated, login is not possible but data is preserved
  • You can reactivate later to continue using the service
  • If you wish to deactivate your account, please contact us at the same email

Contact: dev01@detailgames.ai

If you have any additional questions regarding account deletion, please contact us at any time.
