The Company retains and uses personal information collected for advertising information transmission from the consent collection date until withdrawal of consent. If consent is not withdrawn, personal information of members who have not received advertising information for 1 year continuously will be separately stored or deleted according to relevant laws.

5. Advertising Consent and Right to Refuse

a. Advertising Consent Selection

Members can consent to receive advertising information during membership registration or in marketing information reception settings. Upon consent, advertising information can be received via email, SMS/LMS, and app push notifications.

b. Withdrawal of Advertising Consent

Members can withdraw their consent to receive advertising information at any time. Methods for withdrawing consent are as follows:

  • App Settings: App Menu > Settings > Marketing Information Reception Consent Management
  • Email: Click 'Unsubscribe' link at the bottom of advertising emails
  • Inquiry: Contact support@detailgames.ai
  • Telecom Network Election: Apply through Telecommunications Network Election Management Committee during elections

c. Impact of Refusing Advertising Information

Withdrawing consent to receive advertising information will not limit service use. However, you may not be able to receive information about new promotions and events.

6. Detailed Regulations on Advertising Information Transmission

a. Advertising Information Transmission Policy

  • The Company transmits advertising information only with member consent.
  • Advertising information is transmitted at appropriate frequencies with care not to inconvenience members.
  • Transmitted advertising information consists of content with substantial value for members.
  • Purchase-related information, incentive information, and accurate advertising-related information must be included.

b. Right to Refuse Advertising Information Transmission

  • Members have the right to refuse advertising information reception at any time.
  • The Company immediately stops transmitting advertising information to members who request interruption.
  • When notified that a member refuses advertising information, the Company immediately stops transmitting advertising information to that member.
